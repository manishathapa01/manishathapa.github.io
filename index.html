About Myself

Baglung is a municipality is western Nepal. 275KM west of KTM. The city has located the famous kalika Temple, Kali Gandaki river and panchakot. It is the administrative headquaters of Baglung district and dhaulagiri zone. Baglung services as the major center for business, finance, education and healthcare.
